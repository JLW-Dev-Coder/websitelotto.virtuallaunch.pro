Hurricane Prep

  • Secure windows and doors
  • Emergency water supply (3-day min)
  • Charge all devices, backup power
  • Document insurance details

Tornado Response

  • Move to lowest interior room
  • Stay away from all windows
  • Cover head with heavy blankets
  • NOAA radio for live alerts

Blizzard Survival

  • Layer clothing, protect extremities
  • Heat source + carbon monoxide safety
  • 72-hour food & medication supply
  • Car emergency kit if travelling
