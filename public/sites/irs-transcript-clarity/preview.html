TC 150
Tax Return Filed
Return received and processed by IRS
NORMAL

WHAT IT MEANS

The taxpayer's return has been received and the tax liability has been assessed. This is the starting point of the account timeline.

CLIENT TALKING POINT

"Your return was successfully filed and processed. The IRS has established your account for this tax year."

TC 806
W-2 Withholding Credit
Federal tax withheld from wages
CREDIT

WHAT IT MEANS

This credit reflects federal income tax withheld from the taxpayer's wages, as reported on their W-2.

CLIENT TALKING POINT

"This shows the taxes your employer withheld from your paychecks throughout the year — it's applied as a credit toward what you owe."

TC 846
Refund Issued
Refund check or direct deposit sent
RESOLVED

WHAT IT MEANS

The IRS has issued the refund. Check the date associated with this code for when the deposit or check was sent.

CLIENT TALKING POINT

"Great news — your refund has been issued. If sent via direct deposit, it should arrive within a few business days."

TC 971
Notice Issued
IRS communication or action code
REVIEW

WHAT IT MEANS

A notice has been generated. The action code following TC 971 specifies the type — it could range from an ID verification request to an amended return acknowledgment.

CLIENT TALKING POINT

"The IRS sent you a notice — let's review the specific action code to determine exactly what they need from you."
