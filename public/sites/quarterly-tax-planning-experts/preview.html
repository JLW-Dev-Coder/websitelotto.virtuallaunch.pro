How It Works

How estimated taxes work

The IRS expects quarterly payments if you owe $1,000+ at filing. Here's your roadmap.

1. Estimate Income

Project your annual income from all sources — wages, freelance, investments.

2. Calculate Tax

Apply your effective tax rate to find your total estimated liability.

3. Divide by Four

Split your annual tax into four equal quarterly payments.

4. Pay On Time

Submit each payment by its deadline to avoid penalties and interest.
