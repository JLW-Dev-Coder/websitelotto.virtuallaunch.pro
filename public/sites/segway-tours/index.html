FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior Segway experience?
No experience needed! Every tour begins with a comprehensive training session. Most riders feel confident within 5 minutes.
What's the age and weight limit?
Riders must be at least 12 years old, weigh between 100–260 lbs, and be able to stand unassisted for the tour duration.
What happens if it rains?
Light rain? We ride on! Heavy storms may result in rescheduling at no extra cost. We'll notify you at least 2 hours before your tour.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your tour. Rescheduling is always free and can be done up to 2 hours prior.
