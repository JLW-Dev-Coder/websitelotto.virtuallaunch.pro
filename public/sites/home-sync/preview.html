FAQ

Common questions

How long does installation take?

Basic installs take 1–2 days. Advanced systems typically require 3–5 days. Elite custom installations are scoped individually based on your home's needs.

Do you work with existing devices?

Yes — we integrate with most major smart home brands including Sonos, Ring, Nest, Philips Hue, August, and many more.

What's included in the digital product?

Every tier includes system layout plans, automation flow diagrams, device integration maps, and comprehensive setup guides — delivered digitally upon purchase.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. We design systems to be modular, so upgrading from Basic to Advanced or Elite is seamless with no equipment waste.
