Care Guide

Keep your wig looking fresh. Quick tips + downloadable guide.

Wash Gently

Use sulfate-free shampoo. Lukewarm water only. Pat dry — never wring.

Detangle Smart

Wide-tooth comb from tips to roots. Never brush when wet.

Store Overnight

Use a silk bonnet or wig stand. Keeps shape and prevents tangling.

Heat Protect

Always use heat protectant spray. Keep tools below 350°F for longevity.
