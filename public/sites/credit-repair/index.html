Common Questions

Everything you need to know about credit repair.

How long does credit repair take?
Most clients see significant improvements within 30–90 days. Disputes can take 30–60 days to process with bureaus, but we work with proven strategies to accelerate results.
Is credit repair legal?
Absolutely. The Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA) allows consumers and professionals to dispute inaccurate items on credit reports. We follow all legal guidelines.
Will this hurt my credit further?
No. Disputing errors doesn't damage your credit. In fact, removing inaccurate negative items helps your score recover.
What if I have recent late payments?
We work with current situations. Recent lates reduce impact over time, and we focus on disputing errors and optimizing accounts to rebuild faster.
Can you remove accurate items?
We dispute inaccurate or unverifiable items. Accurate negative accounts naturally age and reduce in impact over time per credit bureau rules.
How much does this cost?
Pricing varies based on complexity. We offer transparent, upfront costs with no hidden fees. Many clients recover the investment through better loan rates alone.
