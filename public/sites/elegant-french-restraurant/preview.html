Our Story

BeauCoCo was born from a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where culinary artistry and Parisian sophistication dance together in perfect harmony.

Founded in 2015, our intimate dining room—bathed in candlelight and adorned with vintage chandeliers—transports guests to the golden age of Parisian bistro culture, reimagined for the modern palate.

Every plate tells a story of seasonal ingredients, meticulous technique, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Every evening is a celebration.

Champagne House

We partner exclusively with small-batch champagne producers, featuring over 40 curated selections to complement every course.
