Ergonomic Design

Setup That Protects You

Posture, lighting, and positioning — the invisible factors that define your long-term productivity.

Monitor Height

Top of screen at eye level. Tilt 10–20° back for a natural gaze angle.

Chair Position

Feet flat on the floor, thighs parallel. Lumbar support aligned with your lower back curve.

Lighting Balance

Ambient + task lighting. No direct glare on screen. 4000K color temperature for sustained focus.

Desk Depth

Minimum 24" depth. Elbows at 90° with wrists neutral on the keyboard surface.
