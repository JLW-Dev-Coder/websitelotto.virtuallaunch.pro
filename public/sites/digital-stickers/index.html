Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know

How do I use these stickers in GoodNotes?

Simply download the PNG files, then import them into GoodNotes using the sticker feature. Drag & drop them onto your pages!

Can I use these for commercial purposes?

Current packs are for personal use only. Commercial/classroom licenses are available separately. Contact us for details!

How long until I get my download?

Instant! Your download link appears immediately after purchase. Check your email within minutes just to be sure.

What's the difference between the packs?

Mini (50 stickers) for basics, Mega (200) for variety, and Classroom Ultimate (500) for teams sharing. All PNG format.

Do these work on Android?

Our stickers are optimized for iPad/GoodNotes, but PNGs work on any device. Android support depends on your app.
