What Triggers Nexus

Understanding Nexus

Nexus is the connection between your business and a state that requires you to collect sales tax. Multiple triggers can create nexus obligations.

Physical Presence

Office, warehouse, employees, or inventory stored in-state. Even temporary presence at trade shows can trigger nexus in some states.

Office Warehouse Employees

Economic Nexus

Exceeding sales revenue or transaction thresholds. Most states set thresholds at $100K in sales or 200 transactions annually.

$100K Revenue 200 Transactions

Marketplace Nexus

Selling through marketplaces like Amazon or Shopify can create obligations. Facilitator laws shift collection duties to platforms in many states.

Amazon Shopify Etsy
