COMMON PENALTIES

Types of IRS Penalties

Understanding what you're dealing with is the first step toward relief.

Failure to File

5% per month on unpaid taxes, up to 25%. Often the most severe — and the most removable.

UP TO 25% PENALTY

Failure to Pay

0.5% per month on unpaid balance. Accrues interest and can compound significantly over time.

0.5% / MONTH

Accuracy Penalty

20% of the underpayment. Applied when the IRS finds substantial errors or negligence on your return.

20% UNDERPAYMENT

Estimated Tax Penalty

Charged when quarterly estimated payments are missed or insufficient throughout the tax year.

QUARTERLY BASED

Information Return

Penalties for late or incorrect W-2s, 1099s, and other information returns filed with the IRS.

PER RETURN

Dishonored Check

2% penalty for payments over $1,250 returned by bank. Even honest mistakes can trigger this.

2% MINIMUM
