Know What You're Facing

Types of IRS Audits

Understanding your audit type is the first step toward building an effective defense strategy.

Correspondence Audit

Conducted entirely by mail. The IRS requests documentation for specific items on your return.

Severity: Moderate

Office Audit

You're asked to appear at a local IRS office with records. Covers broader areas of your return.

Severity: High

Field Audit

An IRS agent visits your home or business. The most thorough and intrusive audit type.

Severity: Critical
